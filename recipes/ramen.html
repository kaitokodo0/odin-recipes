How To Make Ramen

Hey there. Do you like ramen? Well I have a good recipe for you down below!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Bone Broth
  • Vegetables
  • Chicken
  • Seaweed
  • Egg

Instructions

  1. Cook chicken for 20 minutes with preferred sauces or seasonings
  2. Steam the vegetables.
  3. Hard boil the egg.
  4. Boil the noodles in bone broth.
  5. Add the chicken, vegetables and noodles with the broth into a bowl
  6. Add 2 or 3 sheets of seaweed in the ramen. Preferably along the side for style.
  7. Cut the boiled egg in half and place on top of the ramen. Annnd...

Voila! One ramen!
